Martial Power Systems

Martial arts is based on an energy called Qi. It is a basic element for a Cultivator, who weilds Qi, to perform Martial Art Techniques, which is performed through Qi and Qi flows. Qi flow is a pattern of internal flowing Qi that corresponds to a certain effect to the body and can extend further to affect the world. The principal of martial arts is to comprehend the Dao of the world such as time, space, gravity, sound, and reality. There are different categories of Martial Art techniques such as Orthodox, Unorthodox, and those in between. Orthodox techniques build a strong foundation but are slower when compared to Unorthodox techniques of the same rank. Unorthodox are able to speed up cultivation to soar through cultivation stages speedily but it may give problems to their foundation making them weaker that those of that same cultivation stage as them.

The Martial Power System basically is composed of the purity of Qi, storage of Qi, knowledge of techniques, technique performance speed, qi recovery, and qi nature. Generally stories just portray this through ranks or realsm such as Body Tempering Realm, Qi Gathering Realm, Qi Refining Realm, Qi Condensation Realm, Foundation Establishment Realm, Golden Core Realm, Nascent Realm, Earth Realm, Heavenly Realm, Dao Realm and so on. The purity of Qi means techniques can be more effective with less the amount while storage of Qi is the limit of Qi a Cultivator can have. knowledge of techniques are important for performing martial arts of course and technique performance speed is very essential especially in combat. Qi recovery is also important in lasting longer and getting back to peak performance fast.

Cultivators haves different variants such as Weapon Cultivators, who reinforce their weapons and with Qi, weild weapons made out of their own Weapon Qi, and comprehend Weapon Dao. There is also Alchemists, who use Qi to make pills, and Qi Blacksmiths, who use Qi to make Qi equipment. There are also Formation Cultivators who use runes to make arrays and such to make wide-area long lasting effects. Cultivators can be further subdivided by the categories they wield. Although Cultivators can practice of any element or category, there are high disadvantages if the mage does not have an aptitude to that element or category..

The elements can be such as basic ones such as fire, water, earth, or air and some variants such as Yang, metal, Yin, ice, poison, curse, illusion, or nature while there can be advanced elements like gravity, sound, space, time, and reality and some race specific variants such as spirit, gem, body, and blood techniques.
