World Structure System

The world can be set in different ways and the setting could span from a city, continent, world, dimension, realm, universe, time, space, and multiverse depending on the author. Depending on where it is set, the world will have both power, social, and economic structures where power structures easily influence the latter two, be it by lineage or personal strength. The setting could also vary in different reality like, modern, pre-modern, postmodern, eastern, western, victorian, scientific, apocalyptic, and more. The worlds can have diverse characters, humans, elves, fishmen, spirits, monsters, demons, angels, with their various culture and social heirarchy. This whole setting can be heavily influenced by the world's power systems such as mana, qi, and abilities which shifts around the ideas of the setting and is a key concept added to not only the socio-economic structure but also the cuture and heirarchy.
